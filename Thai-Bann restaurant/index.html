Larb Moo

"Larb Moo" is a delicious and fragrant Thai dish. It is infused with the spiciness of chili and various herbs, creating a rich and exciting flavor. For customers who enjoy spicy tastes, our restaurant enhances the dish with additional chili and herbs at the end. This adds a distinctive and unique character to the flavor of our larb, setting it apart from others in the market.

Special Selection

Delicious Menu

  • Khao Man Gai

    Seasonal 60B

    Khao Man Gai is a dish where fragrant rice is served with boiled chicken. It's often accompanied by a sweet and savory dipping sauce.

  • Khao Pad

    55B

    Thai fried rice is a delightful mix of flavors, combining either pork, chicken, or shrimp with eggs, tomatoes, and onions. It's typically served with cucumber slices and lime wedges.

  • Kuay Teow Nam Tok

    80B

    Kuay Teow Nam Tok is a Thai noodle soup dish made with flat rice noodles served in a flavorful, spicy, and tangy broth with tender meat slices.

  • Som Tum

    New 75B

    Som Tum is a spicy Thai salad made with shredded green papaya, tomatoes, peanuts, lime, and chili, all pounded together in a mortar. It offers a perfect balance of flavors, including sweetness, sourness, and spiciness.

  • Tod Mun Goongs

    30B

    Tod Mun Goong is a popular Thai appetizer made from ground shrimp, fish, or pork mixed with crispy fried batter. They are seasoned with salt, fragrant herbs, and spices, offering a salty, aromatic, and mildly spicy taste.

  • Pad Krapow

    60B

    Pad Krapow is a Thai stir-fry dish made with fresh basil leaves, minced meat (pork, chicken, or duck), and chilies. It's known for its aromatic basil flavor and spiciness.
